Our Vision

Our Mission

Shopping online shouldn't feel like a chore. We created PricePact because finding the best price often means visiting multiple stores, comparing shipping costs and wondering if a better offer exists elsewhere. Our goal is simple: bring trusted retailers together in one place, make prices transparent and help everyone shop with confidence. We don't sell products ourselves—we help you discover the right retailer at the right price.

Starting Local. Thinking Global.

Every successful platform starts somewhere. PricePact will launch first in the Netherlands, allowing us to perfect the experience, build relationships with trusted retailers and ensure our comparison engine delivers reliable results. Once we've built a solid foundation, we'll gradually expand into new markets across Europe before exploring opportunities worldwide.

How PricePact Works

1. Search

Search for the product you're looking for.

2. Compare

Compare prices from trusted retailers in your country.

3. Purchase

Complete your purchase directly on the retailer's official website.

Transparent by Design

PricePact never acts as the seller. We connect shoppers with trusted retailers and redirect you to their official websites to complete your purchase securely. In some cases, we may earn a small affiliate commission from participating retailers. This never changes the price you pay and never influences which retailer offers the best deal.
